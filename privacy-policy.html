Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 09, 2026

Welcome to Raining Balls. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled while using the game.

1. Information Collection

Raining Balls does not directly collect, store or share personal information. The game does not require an account, login or any personal information such as your name, email address or phone number. Game settings and high scores may be stored locally on your device to provide normal gameplay.

2. Advertising

Raining Balls uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may automatically collect technical information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Ad interaction information

These data are processed exclusively by Google according to its own privacy policy.

3. In-App Purchases

Currently, Raining Balls does not offer in-app purchases. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Children's Privacy

Raining Balls is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Security

Since no personal information is stored by the developer, there is no personal database maintained by Raining Balls.

6. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the application, third-party services or legal requirements.
